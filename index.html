Clothing We Offer

We offer a variety of authentic Pakistani pieces! From one-piece kurtis to two-piece suits and even accessories!

Kurtis

Our one piece kurtis come in small, medium and large sizes. We offer many different types of fabric types, including khaddar and lilen. They come in authentic embroidered and printed designs - and are only of the best quality.

Full Suits

Our full suits can either come as 2-piece; which includes a trouser and shirt, or a 3 piece; which includes a trouser, shirt and dupatta. Much like our kurtis they come in all different types of fabric and designs. They are guaranteed to be of high quality and long-lasting!

Acessories

If you're not looking for any clothes, we also offer quality jewelry. Anything from churiya, to rings, necklaces or simple bracelets, our accessories will definitely compliment not only our clothing but for casual wearing as well!
